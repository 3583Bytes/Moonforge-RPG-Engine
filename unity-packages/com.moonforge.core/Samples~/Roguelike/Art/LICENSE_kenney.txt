	The 16x16 sprites in Resources/Sprites/ were cropped from two
	Kenney packs, both CC0:

	1) 1-Bit Pack (1.2)
	   - Source: https://kenney.nl/assets/1-bit-pack
	   - Used for: characters (hero, enemy, enemy_elite, enemy_boss,
	     npc), the town guard marker, and stairs (stairs_down,
	     stairs_up).

	2) Roguelike RPG Pack
	   - Source: https://kenney.nl/assets/roguelike-rpg-pack
	   - Used for: floors (town_floor, dungeon_floor), doors
	     (town_door), pillars (dungeon_pillar), and most town
	     markers (marker_fountain, marker_shrine, marker_cache,
	     marker_shop, marker_healer, marker_alchemist,
	     marker_questboard).

	Only the cropped per-sprite PNGs are bundled — download the packs
	from the URLs above if you want to crop additional tiles.

	Both packs are by Kenney (www.kenney.nl), with help on the
	Roguelike pack by Lynn Evers (@EversLynn).
